# Road-Accident-Analysis
# Road Accident Analysis Dashboard (With YoY Trends)

##📌Project Overview
This project was developed as part of the **Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI)** training to analyze road safety data and identify critical risk factors.The dashboard focuses on **Current Year (CY)** performance compared to the **Previous Year (PY)** to track safety improvements and casualty trends.

##📊Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks five primary metrics with **Year-over-Year (YoY)** growth indicators.
*Total CY Casualties:** 195.7K (a **11.9% decrease** YoY)
*Total CY Accidents:** 144.4K (a **11.7% decrease** YoY)
*CY Fatal Casualties:** 2.9K (significant **33.3% reduction** YoY).
*CY Serious Casualties:** 27.0K (a **16.2% decrease** YoY)
*CY Slight Casualties:** 165.8K (a **10.6% decrease** YoY).

##🔍Analytical Insights
*Urban vs. Rural:** **61.2%** of casualties occur in Urban areas, while Rural areas account for **38.8%**.
*Peak Times:** **Friday** recorded the highest volume of casualties (**68K**), followed by Wednesday and Tuesday.
*Road & Light Conditions:** The majority of accidents occur on **Single Carriageways** and during **Daylight** (**73%**).

##🛠️Technical Stack
* **Power BI Desktop:** Dashboard design and visualization.
* **Power Query:** Data cleaning, handling data types, and normalization.
* **DAX:** Time Intelligence functions for CY and PY comparisons.
